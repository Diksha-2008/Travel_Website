The Maldives is a tropical paradise in the Indian Ocean, known for its crystal-cl ear waters, white sandy beaches, and vibrant coral reefs. Fiji

Fiji is an archipelago in the South Pacific, consisting of over 330 islands. Known for its stunning beaches, clear lagoons, and coral reefs, it offers a rich culture, vibrant wildlife, and tropical climate. Popular for water sports, adventure activities, and luxury resorts.

California

California is a diverse state on the west coast of the U.S., known for its beaches, mountains, and vibrant cities. Home to landmarks like Hollywood, Yosemite, and the Golden Gate Bridge, it boasts a thriving tech industry, agriculture, and a rich cultural scene.

Delhi

Delhi, India's capital, is a bustling metropolis blending history and modernity. It boasts iconic landmarks like the Red Fort, India Gate, and Qutub Minar. Known for its rich culture, vibrant markets, and diverse cuisine, Delhi serves as a political, historical, and economic hub, offering a dynamic urban experience.

Baali

Baali, a character from Hindu mythology, is a powerful monkey king mentioned in the epic Ramayana. Known for his immense strength and valor, Baali ruled Kishkindha and was defeated by Lord Rama with the help of his brother Sugriva. His story reflects themes of loyalty, power, and justice. One of the most preferred tourist destination all around the world.
